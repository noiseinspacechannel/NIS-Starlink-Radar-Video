About This Demo

Compare real overhead satellite imagery at various resolutions. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan, use the slider to simulate resolution degradation.

🛰️ Optical — Maxar WorldView-2

  • Resolution: 30 cm GSD
  • Location: Los Angeles, CA (Wildfires Jan 2025)
  • Coverage: ~2.46 km × 2.46 km
  • License: CC-BY-NC-4.0

📡 SAR — Umbra UMBRA-05

  • Resolution: 35 cm GEC
  • Location: Port of Long Beach, CA
  • Coverage: ~2.87 km × 2.87 km
  • Band: X-band (VV Polarization)
  • License: CC-BY-4.0

How It Works

Lower resolution levels are created by average-filter downsampling the native imagery, then nearest-neighbor upscaling to show what coarser sensors would see.
